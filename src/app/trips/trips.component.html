Spring 2024 Trips:

Climbing at the Bluffs

Outdoor climbing at the Bluffs!! Located right here in Clarksville, it is the perfect opportunity to get introduced to Outdoor climbing. And IT'S FREE, register before 5pm the day before we meet!

We are going to Indian GravePoint Cave, just South of Nashville, to do some caving! This is a great opportunity to get an introduction into spelunking, at the low rate of $25. We will provide transportation, gear, and support throughout the trip!

Join us at the gorgeous Red River, located 15 minutes from campus in Adams, TN! We will provide instructions, equipment, and boats to ensure you are properly prepared! Allow your stress from this semester to melt away as you float down the beautiful river. All of this for only $25, Register now!

Foster Falls Rock Climbing

Wanting to take the next step in your rock climbing journey? Join us at Foster Falls for a great evening of challenging and fun routes! $65 for this amazing adventure is worth every penny!
